# The AI Sovereignty Act (DRAFT)
March 2026

## Section 1: Data Localization
All multi-modal foundation models deployed within national borders must be trained exclusively on localized data shards. This ensures that the cultural and linguistic nuances of our citizens are not diluted by generalized global weights.

## Section 2: Algorithmic Accountability
Every model update exceeding 10^24 FLOPS requires a mandatory 30-day "Social Impact Assessment." Developers must prove that the new weights do not exhibit harmful bias against local minority groups.

## Section 3: Sovereignty Fees
A 2.5% "Computational Levy" will be applied to all cloud GPU compute sessions. These funds will be redirected to the National AI Utility to support domestic open-source alternatives.
